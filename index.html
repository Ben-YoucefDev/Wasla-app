Common questions

Is the APK safe to install?
Yes. The Wasla APK is the official release built directly from the app's source code. Installing from APK is a standard and secure method on Android devices.
Does Wasla work outside Algeria?
Wasla is exclusively designed for use within Algeria. All location searches, driver operations, and pricing are calibrated for the Algerian market.
How do I update the app?
Simply return to this website and download the latest APK when a new version is available. Install it over the existing app — your account data will be preserved.
What Android version is required?
Wasla requires Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or newer. Most smartphones from 2016 and later are fully compatible.
How do I become a driver?
Download the app, select "Register as Driver" during sign-up, and complete your profile. Our authorizer team reviews applications and verifies drivers before they can go online.
